## Disqualifiers

- **Headline-only move:** one-session rallies driven by headlines without volume/follow-through remain `watch_only`.

- **Repeated early-window weakness:** given posterior T+3/T+5 misses, any setup lacking near-term timing evidence must stay `watch_only` even if the medium-term story sounds plausible.
- **Broken support with rising volume:** candidates breaking recent support on strong turnover move toward `avoid`, while explicitly acknowledging near-term rebound risk.
- **Unclear invalidation:** any idea without a concrete invalidation level cannot be upgraded.
- **Position sizing conflict:** no single position may exceed 10% and no theme may exceed 30%; if several tech names trigger together, prefer ETF confirmation or one best candidate rather than stacking correlated exposure.
