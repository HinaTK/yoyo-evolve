# Investment Error Patterns

## Patterns To Track
- Overweighting fresh headlines without price confirmation.
- Ignoring weak ETF confirmation while upgrading a single stock.
- Turning a swing thesis into a long-term hold without explicit review.
- Confusing volatility with momentum.
- Issuing blunt `avoid` calls that are early at T+3/T+5 even when later direction is correct.
- Issuing clustered `avoid` calls across correlated ETFs/names when broad rebound-risk evidence is unavailable.
- Treating a correct T+10/T+20 outcome as proof that T+3/T+5 timing was acceptable.
- Letting posterior outcomes stand in for live market confirmation.
- Upgrading low-pass-rate symbols to `buy_candidate`, `hold`, or `accumulate` on single-name evidence while broad/ETF confirmation is absent.
- Issuing `avoid` on rebound-prone or low-pass-rate symbols without live downside confirmation.
- Treating a selective, low-volume risk-on rebound below MA60 as enough confirmation to upgrade rebound-prone tech exposure.
- Upgrading broad-index ETFs after recent bullish failures without breadth, volume, and moving-average confirmation.
