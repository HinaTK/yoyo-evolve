# Active Investment Learnings

This file stores the currently active investment learnings that should influence the next research cycle.

## Current Focus
- Require ETF confirmation before upgrading single-name internet platform trades.
- Default to `watch_only` when market regime is `risk_off` and the setup is momentum-dependent.
- Separate short-term timing confidence from medium-term thesis confidence after repeated T+3/T+5 timing misses.
- Before issuing `avoid`, check explicit T+3/T+5 rebound risk against the T+10/T+20 thesis.
- Before issuing clustered `avoid` calls across correlated HK tech/internet names, check broad/ETF rebound risk first; if unavailable, downgrade to `watch_only`.
- Make horizon separation the first checklist item when posterior data shows early-window misses.
- Use posterior evidence to reduce confidence, not as a substitute for live confirmation.
- After repeated bullish misreads, require broad-market plus ETF confirmation before upgrading low-pass-rate symbols to `buy_candidate`, `hold`, or `accumulate`.
- After repeated defensive misreads, require live downside confirmation plus broad/ETF rebound-risk checks before issuing `avoid` on rebound-prone or low-pass-rate symbols.
- On selective risk-on days with weak volume and prices still below MA60, keep rebound-prone tech ETFs and single names at `watch_only`.
- Do not upgrade broad-index ETFs after recent failed bullish calls unless breadth, volume, and moving-average confirmation all improve.
